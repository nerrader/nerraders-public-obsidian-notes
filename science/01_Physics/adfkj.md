## 1. Physics: The "Rules" of the Universe

Physics is the study of matter, energy, and how they interact. It’s the foundation for everything else.

- **Forces & Motion:** * Newton’s Three Laws of Motion.
    
    - Types of forces: Gravity, Friction, Air Resistance, Magnetism.
        
    - Speed, Velocity, and Acceleration.
        
- **Energy:**
    
    - Kinetic vs. Potential energy.
        
    - Law of Conservation of Energy (Energy cannot be created or destroyed).
        
    - Energy transfers (Heat, Light, Sound, Electricity).
        
- **Waves:**
    
    - The Electromagnetic Spectrum (Light, Radio, X-rays).
        
    - Properties of waves: Amplitude, Wavelength, Frequency.
        
    - How sound travels through different mediums.
        
- **Electricity & Magnetism:**
    
    - Circuits (Series vs. Parallel).
        
    - Voltage, Current, and Resistance ($V=IR$).
