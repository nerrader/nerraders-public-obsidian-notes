## 3. Biology: The "Living Systems"

Biology applies physics and chemistry to living organisms to see how they survive and reproduce.

- **Cell Biology:**
    
    - Cell Theory (All living things are made of cells).
        
    - Organelles (Mitochondria, Nucleus, Ribosomes).
        
    - Plant vs. Animal cells.
        
    - Cell Division (Mitosis and Binary Fission).
        
- **Human Body Systems:**
    
    - Circulatory, Respiratory, Digestive, Nervous, and Skeletal systems.
        
    - How these systems interact to maintain **Homeostasis**.
        
- **Genetics & Evolution:**
    
    - DNA structure (The Double Helix).
        
    - Inheritance (Punnett Squares, Dominant vs. Recessive traits).
        
    - Natural Selection and Adaptation.
        
- **Ecology:**
    
    - Food Chains and Food Webs.
        
    - Ecosystems and Biomes.
        
    - The Carbon and Nitrogen Cycles.
