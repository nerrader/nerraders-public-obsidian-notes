These organisms are only made of one cell, they are very different from other organisms, so they are placed in their own kingdom

Categories: Protozoa, algae and molds

Example of each type:
Amoeboids (Protozoa), Diatoms (algae), Aspergillus (Mold)

Protocists can make their own food using sunlight, this process also produces oxygen. They are also an important part of the food chain as many small organisms eat protists. Some protists are useful
