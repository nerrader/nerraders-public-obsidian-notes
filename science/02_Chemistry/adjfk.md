## 2. Chemistry: The "Building Blocks"

Chemistry looks at what things are made of and how they change when they combine.

- **Matter:**
    
    - States of Matter (Solid, Liquid, Gas, Plasma) and Phase Changes.
        
    - Atoms: Protons, Neutrons, and Electrons.
        
    - Elements, Compounds, and Mixtures.
        
- **The Periodic Table:**
    
    - Atomic Number and Atomic Mass.
        
    - Groups (columns) and Periods (rows).
        
    - Metals vs. Non-metals.
        
- **Chemical Reactions:**
    
    - Reactants and Products.
        
    - Conservation of Mass (Balancing equations).
        
    - Exothermic (releases heat) vs. Endothermic (absorbs heat) reactions.
        
    - Acids and Bases (The pH scale).
        
- **Bonding (Intro level):**
    
    - Ionic vs. Covalent bonding (how atoms "stick" together).
